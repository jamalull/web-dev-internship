Programme We Offer

This program can help you improve career success, and the best quality of education in Indonesia.

Diploma

The process of admitting new students for the Vocational Education program is carried out through the PPKB and SIMAK UI routes. The Vocational Education Program organizes Associate Expert (D3) and Applied Bachelor (D4) levels

Bachelor's

The University of Indonesia Undergraduate Program has several types of classes, namely: Regular, Parallel, International and Extension Classes.

Magister

The Masters program is a program for students who have graduated from an undergraduate program. The aim of this program is to enhance skills, knowledge and career. The entry route for the master program is through SIMAK UI for postgraduate programs.

Doctor

The Doctoral Program (S3) is a strata 3 education program (S3) aimed at obtaining the academic title doctor (Dr) as the highest academic degree in the doctoral program.

Specialist

Specialist is a higher education level as a continuation of professional education which aims to achieve steady professional mastery in a particular field based on competent academic ability.

Profesion

Professional education is a higher education program after an undergraduate education program that prepares students to have jobs with special skills requirements.

Our Facilities Campus

Facilities at Indonesian universities are very complete, consisting of libraries, sports stadiums, mosques, laboratories, and others.

UI Library

The Library of the University of Indonesia is one of the main supporting units for academic activities (learning, teaching and research). The UI Library has around 1.5 million collections stored in the UI Library and in several faculty libraries.

Hospital

Transportation
